# Phase I — Pathology communication safety

A pathology case cannot close merely because a clinician reviewed the result. Closure requires a controlled communication disposition.

Supported dispositions include approved-report delivery, direct contact, follow-up review, no-notification-required, patient declined, unable to contact, transfer to external care, and duplicate cancellation. Each option has explicit evidence rules; unable-to-contact requires at least three attempts and human-owned dispositions require a note.

The decision, note, attempts, actor, and timestamp are stored and audited. Demo delivery does not satisfy a confirmed-delivery disposition.
