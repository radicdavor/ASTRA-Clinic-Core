# Phase F — Endoscopy forms

Published synthetic gastroenterology form versions bind consultation, gastroscopy, and colonoscopy services to separate clinical records and separate signed reports.

Gastroscopy captures structured oesophageal, gastric, and duodenal findings, multiple biopsy sites, interventions, specimens, and immediate complications. Colonoscopy captures extent, terminal ileum, bowel-preparation quality, segment findings, repeatable polyps, polypectomy/biopsy/clip interventions, withdrawal time, specimens, and complications.

The generic legacy “Status” field is not used as a substitute for endoscopy structure. Published schemas are immutable; changes require a new reviewed version.
