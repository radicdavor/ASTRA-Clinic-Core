# Program 1 Phase E4 - Review Human Responsibility Role Boundary

Status: documented

## Human Responsibility

Review requires accountable human interpretation. Physician responsibility governs clinical interpretation and downstream decisions.

## Role Boundaries

- Physician: may interpret clinical meaning in future governed workflows.
- Nurse: may support review context but does not create physician decisions.
- Receptionist: no clinical interpretation.
- Admin: operational access does not imply clinical authority.
- AI/API key: denied by default for review action.
- System job: denied by default for review action.

## AI Boundary

AI suggestions are not official truth and cannot complete review, diagnosis, treatment, approval, clearance or override.
