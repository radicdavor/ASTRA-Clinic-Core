# Program 1 Phase D6 - Findings Recommendation and Decision Boundary

Status: recommendation/decision boundary

## Purpose

This document separates recommendations, physician decisions and automated suggestions.

## Automated Suggestion

AI or system logic may later suggest a possible recommendation draft.

That draft is not official clinical truth.

It must remain clearly marked as a suggestion until reviewed and confirmed by an authorized clinician.

## Recommendation

A recommendation is a proposed clinical direction.

It may be:

- physician-authored
- physician-confirmed
- draft/pending review

A recommendation is not patient instruction unless separately approved for patient-facing communication.

## Physician Decision

A physician decision is a separate concept from a finding.

It should record an explicit human clinical judgment, reason and source context under future governance.

## No Automatic Actions

Findings and recommendations must not automatically create:

- treatment plan
- patient instruction
- follow-up scheduling
- referral
- patient messaging
- Task
- Outcome Evidence
- appointment status mutation

## Boundary Examples

- A finding may suggest that follow-up should be considered.
- A recommendation draft may state that physician review is needed.
- A physician decision may later document the chosen path.
- None of those steps may happen automatically in D0-D10.

## Runtime Boundary

D6 is documentation-only.
