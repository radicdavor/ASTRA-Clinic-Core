# Program 1 Phase X3 - Non-Production Continuity Decision

Decision: continue Program 1 as non-production.

## Decision rationale

The completed Program 1 governance/prototype phases document strong boundaries, but they do not close production, real-data, PHI/PII, validation, clinical deployment, operational readiness, or go-live blockers.

Negative tests and validation harness outputs remain non-production and passive. They are prohibitions and evidence expectations, not features or approvals.

## Continuity conditions

Future Program 1 work must continue to preserve:

- no production approval
- no real patient data approval
- no PHI/PII processing approval
- no autonomous diagnosis or treatment
- no clinical write workflows
- no patient communication
- no appointment mutation
- no workflow enforcement
- no runtime approval/clearance/override behavior
- no go-live authorization

Phase X introduces no new runtime capability.
