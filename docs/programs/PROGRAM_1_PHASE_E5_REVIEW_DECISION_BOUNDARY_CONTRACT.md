# Program 1 Phase E5 - Review Decision Boundary Contract

Status: documented

Review may precede a physician decision, but decision requires separate human confirmation. Review does not automatically decide diagnosis, treatment, follow-up, referral, patient instruction, patient message, Outcome Evidence or Task.

## Required Separation

Future systems must represent review and decision as separate concepts. A reviewed status must not be displayed as clinical approval, clearance, resolution or treatment choice.
