Design the explicit governance controls required before any future production-readiness claim, including responsibility boundaries, review gates, non-approval controls, safety language, and escalation requirements.

`Program 1 Phase N0 - Pilot Demo Operations Pack`

Purpose:

`Program 1 Phase N0 - Governance Control Design`

Rationale:

After Phase L identifies gaps and Phase M sequences remediation, the safest next step is to design governance controls before expanding demo operations. This prevents demo readiness from drifting into implied production readiness.
