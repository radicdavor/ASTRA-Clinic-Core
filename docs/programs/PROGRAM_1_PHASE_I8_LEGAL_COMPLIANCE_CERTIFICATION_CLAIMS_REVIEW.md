# Program 1 Phase I8 - Legal Compliance Certification Claims Review

## Claims That Must Not Be Made

- ASTRA is not a certified EMR.
- ASTRA is not a certified medical device.
- ASTRA is not approved for production clinical use.
- ASTRA is not approved for real patient data.
- ASTRA does not automate diagnosis.
- ASTRA does not automate treatment.
- ASTRA does not automate clinical decisions.

## Required Before Real Use

- legal/compliance review
- Croatian and GDPR review
- DPIA where required
- clinical safety review
- data processing and retention approval
- access-control approval
- incident response approval

## Decision

Any production, real-data, EMR, medical-device or clinical decision claim remains no-go.
