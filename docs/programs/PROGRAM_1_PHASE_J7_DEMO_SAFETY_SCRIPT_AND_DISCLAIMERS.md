# Program 1 Phase J7 - Demo Safety Script and Disclaimers

## Opening Disclaimer

This is a closed pilot demo using synthetic demo data only.

## Clinical Safety Disclaimer

The system shows source-linked context. It does not diagnose, treat, approve, clear or replace clinician judgment.

## Demo-Data Disclaimer

No real patient names, identifiers, clinical documents, contact details or messages may be entered.

## No-Decision Disclaimer

Timeline events, findings, open questions, readiness snapshots and acknowledgments are not final clinical decisions.

## No-Production Disclaimer

This release candidate is not production-ready.

## No-Certification Disclaimer

ASTRA Clinic Core is not a certified EMR and is not a certified medical device.

## Closing Disclaimer

Future production or real-data use requires separate legal, security, compliance, access-control, audit, backup/restore and clinician approval.
