# Program 1 Phase I9 - Usability Review Governance

## Surfaces Requiring Review

- findings workspace
- timeline workspace
- readiness preview and snapshot wording
- acknowledgment wording
- empty/error/permission states
- source-linked limitations/provenance display

## Review Questions

- Do clinicians understand that read-only records are not clinical decisions?
- Do nurses/receptionists understand role limits?
- Are no-action boundaries clear?
- Are forbidden interpretations avoided?
- Are missing-source and error states safe?
- Are demo/pilot limitations visible enough?

## Production Blockers

Production and real-data use require documented pilot feedback, role training, usability issue triage and maintainer approval.
