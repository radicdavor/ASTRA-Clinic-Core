# Program 1 Architecture Review Track Phase E6 - Patient Instruction and Communication Prohibition

Status: documentation-only prohibition record.

## Prohibited patient-facing actions

Phase E does not permit:

- Patient messaging.
- Patient reminders.
- Patient education delivery.
- Patient portal output.
- Automatic patient summaries.
- Staff-mediated automatic patient instruction.
- Treatment instructions to patients.
- Preparation instructions to patients.
- Appointment instructions to patients.

## Current decision

Patient instruction and communication remain prohibited. Phase E does not create direct or indirect patient-facing workflows, staff-mediated patient instruction, patient messaging, appointment messaging, treatment communication, preparation communication, or patient portal output.
