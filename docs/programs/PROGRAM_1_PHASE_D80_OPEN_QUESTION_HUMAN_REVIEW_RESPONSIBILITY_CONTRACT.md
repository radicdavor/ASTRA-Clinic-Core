# Program 1 Phase D80 - Open Question Human Review Responsibility Contract

Status: human review contract

## Purpose

Define who may interpret open questions.

## Responsibilities

Physicians are responsible for clinical interpretation.

Nurses may support preparation and context review only where future policy allows.

Reception is limited to administrative/source availability context.

AI agents, API keys and system jobs may not make official interpretations.

## Review Rules

Review is required before an open question may influence clinical interpretation.

Review does not automatically create:

- physician decision
- recommendation
- Task
- Outcome Evidence
- patient message
- appointment status mutation
- diagnosis
- treatment plan

## Future Audit Expectations

Future audit should record actor, role, timestamp, source references, question key and review note/reason.

Audit must not imply Outcome Evidence.
